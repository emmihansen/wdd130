Purpose

Our purpose here at the Dry Oar Rafting Co. is create an outdoor experience for all the the family enjoy. We take groups out on the beautiful Salmon River to experience nature from the view of the river. Our adventures have enough rapids to keep things exciting and fun while also having enough calm river float time to take in the scenery. Are you looking for a retreat that the whole family can enjoy? We operate on several sections of the Salmon River that offer variety of experiences that can tailored towards your groups desired experience. Can't find an comprehensive getway rafting experience? Look no further than Dry Oar! Our company provides lodging at several local locations nearby with a discount for those who book through us. We provide equipment and gear, transportation services, nature tours, and certified rafting guides. What if we have never been on rafting before? Don't worry, we have you covered! Our certified guides provide training sessions for beginneers.
