History

Our company was founded in 2004 by Ryan and Rebecca Hansen. They loved river and they loved their family, and they had delightful memories of taking their kids on the river for some fun. When all their kids had grown up and left the area, the Hansen's decided they wanted to bring that run river rafting memories to other families. This was motivating factor behind Dry Oars rafting company.
